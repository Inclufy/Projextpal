## Tollgate / governance violations (highest priority)
1. **Tollgate does not gate.** `sixsigma/views.py:670-680` `approve()` sets `approved=True`, `status='passed'`, stamps a date ‚ÄĒ and nothing else. It does **not** verify the phase's deliverables exist (no check for an approved charter, a CTQ tree, a captured baseline, a control plan) and does **not** lock the next phase's data entry. Any phase's data can be created at any time. `checklist_completed`/`deliverables_reviewed` fields (`models.py:1093-1094`) exist but are never enforced. ‚ĚĆ A tollgate that holds status but blocks nothing is governance theatre.
2. **Charter approval is unconditional.** `sixsigma/views.py:167-176` `approve()` flips `approved=True` with no required-field or business-case completeness check, and no link to whether Define may "close". ‚ö†ÔłŹ
3. **No savings/ROI validation gate.** `ProjectClosure.approve` (`views.py:721-727`) just records `approved_by`; there is no validated-financial-benefit field reconciled against the charter's target, and no Champion sign-off distinct from the author. ‚ĚĆ Benefits are unvalidated.
4. **Two parallel, unsynced metric stores.** `lss_green.LSSGreenMetric` (Cp/Cpk/DPMO/sigma) and `lss_green.LSSGreenMeasurement` are **manual-entry only** ‚ÄĒ Cp/Cpk are typed into the form (`LSSGreenMetrics.tsx:119-120`, `parseFloat` at `:48`) and stored raw (`lss_green/models.py:107-110`). Nothing computes capability from spec limits + process ŌÉ. Meanwhile `sixsigma.BaselineMetric` is a third, separate store. A Green Belt has three disconnected places to put numbers and none reconcile. ‚ö†ÔłŹ/‚ĚĆ
5. **DMAICPhase.status is decorative.** `lss_green/models.py:25-32` `markComplete` (`LSSGreenPhases.tsx:80-85`) sets `status='completed'` with zero precondition ‚ÄĒ no tollgate link, no deliverable check.

**Operability headline:** A Green Belt *can* run the full DMAIC walk end-to-end on real pages with real persistence ‚ÄĒ this is far more operable than the baseline assumed. The two genuine analytical computations are **SPC control limits** (`recalculate_limits`, real ŌÉ-based UCL/LCL + auto violation detection) and the Fishbone vote/root-cause workflow. The gaps are (a) **capability (Cp/Cpk) is never calculated** anywhere ‚ÄĒ purely typed, and (b) the thin `lss_green` Metrics/Measurements pages duplicate `sixsigma` with no compute and no reconciliation.

## Feature proposals + redesign (ranked ‚ÄĒ both directions)
1. **Enforce the tollgate gate (top priority, weight=Cross-cutting √ó high value).** Add a `can_pass()` check in `TollgateReview.approve` that verifies the phase's required deliverables exist+approved (Define‚Üícharter.approved & SIPOC & ‚Č•1 CTQ; Measure‚Üíbaseline captured & data-collection plan; Analyze‚Üí‚Č•1 validated root cause; Improve‚Üípilot+before/after metric; Control‚Üícontrol plan+chart). Block next-phase POSTs until the prior tollgate `status='passed'`. Reuse the existing `checklist_completed`/`deliverables_reviewed` fields. *Low effort, models already exist.*
2. **Compute capability.** Add `compute_capability` action: derive Cp = (USL‚ąíLSL)/(6ŌÉ) and Cpk from a metric's spec limits + the std-dev of its measurement series (or a control chart's data points), persisting onto a single canonical metric. Add `sigma_level`/DPMO derivation from yield. Wire from the Measure baseline page.
3. **Collapse the duplicate stores.** Deprecate `lss_green.LSSGreenMetric` + `LSSGreenMeasurement` in favour of `sixsigma.BaselineMetric` + a capability record, or make `lss_green` a read-through view of `sixsigma`. Three unsynced number stores is the worst confusion for a practitioner.
4. **Savings validation ledger + Champion sign-off.** Extend `ProjectClosure` with `validated_savings`, `validation_method`, and a Champion `approve` distinct from the author; reconcile against `ProjectCharter` target before allowing Control tollgate to pass. (Cross-link with Black Belt module ‚ÄĒ shared DMAIC spine.)
5. **Link DMAICPhase ‚ÜĒ TollgateReview.** Drive `DMAICPhase.status` from the tollgate state so the thin dashboard and the governed gate agree, eliminating the decorative `markComplete`.
